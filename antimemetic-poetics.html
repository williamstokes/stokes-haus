antimemetic poetics

The following poems contain antimemetic properties and are contained within an encrypted file structure. They are protected by a password, the epigraph of each poem functions as the password hint. The reader may use memetic techniques to decode the epigraph, but should note that the passwords themselves are also antimemetic entities - it is recommended to think obliquely, and judiciously - as there is a maximum number of attempts per day. The more difficult the password, the more attempts are offered. If the reader cannot access the poem and runs out of attempts, they reset each midnight Eastern Standard Time. When the poem is revealed and read, the reader must consent to forget it, and the page will reset.
