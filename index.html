Will Stokes is an MFA candidate in poetry at the Bennington Writing Seminars.

He studied at Vanderbilt University and the Berlin Writers' Workshop. He lives in Brooklyn.

His work explores the intersections of place, memory, and the natural world, often set against the backdrop of the American South.
