Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 19, 2025

Information Collection

This website is a personal poetry portfolio. I collect minimal information necessary to understand how visitors interact with the site.

Google Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics. Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to track page views, user interactions, and other standard web analytics data.

I have enabled IP anonymization in Google Analytics, which means your IP address is anonymized before being sent to Google's servers.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Local Storage

Some features of this website (specifically, the "antimemetic poetics" section) use browser local storage to track password attempt counts. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any server. This data is used solely to enforce daily attempt limits and resets automatically at midnight Eastern Time.

Data Sharing

I do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Google Analytics data is subject to Google's privacy policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact me through the contact information provided on this website.
